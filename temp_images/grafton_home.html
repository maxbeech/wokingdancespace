Welcome to Grafton Tennis and Squash Club

The Grafton Tennis and Squash Club is a community club run by a groupa passionate volunteersproudlyserving people ofall backgrounds, mainlyliving in theBalham, Streathamand Claphamareas. The club is a quiet green oasis in South London where you can play the sports you love and enjoy a leisurely drink or two afterwards. We welcome tennis and squash players of all standards and age groups, and are very family-oriented with strong junior sections and competitive tennis and squash county teams.

We offer 8 floodlit all weather tennis courts (4 hard, 4 artificial clay) in a tree-lined green space, 3 high-spec squash courts with modern LED lighting, a spacious garden area and patio, fully-stocked bar, online booking system, changing rooms with showers, and a large clubhouse with 2 table tennis tables.

We pride ourselves on being welcoming and friendly with an active social calendar of activities throughout the year.We adhere to a strict equality policy and maintain the club to ensure a safe environment for everyone.

We are located near Streatham at 70a Thornton Road, SW12 0LF, just 3 minutes walk from Numbers 50 and 255 bus stops on Kings Avenue. We are 10-15 minutes walk from Streatham Hill station or 15-20 minutes walk from Clapham South and Balham tube stations. We also have a large car park.

So drop in to check us out, contact us for more information or join our club today.
